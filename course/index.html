Internal Review
What to keep, what to refine
An honest read on the delivery — what landed, and the small bets we’d place differently next time for India-based clients.
✓ What worked — keep doing
~1h 15m of hands-on build
Despite a late start, most learners had a working bot running by the end — the build window held.
Instructor depth & delivery
Knowledge and Teaching both scored 4.75/5 — the highest dimensions. Quoted directly in 6 of 8 written responses.
Conceptual transfer
Pre → Post lift of 25pp with 4 learners hitting a perfect score — the RAG mental model landed.
↻ To refine next time
Default to in-person for India clients
Fully offline delivery would lift interactivity (4.13) and empowerment (4.25) — our two lowest dimensions.
Trim the orientation runway
First 30–40 min was light on teaching; attention typically dips ~1h in. Compress orientation and pull the first build moment forward.
Hold the start time
A 15–20 min late start ate into hands-on time. Tighter pre-class checks and a hard 0:00 kickoff protect the build window.
